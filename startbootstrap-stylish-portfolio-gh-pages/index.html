How It Works

Effortless Feedback Collection That Actually Works

VoiceRevu allows customers to leave feedback via WhatsApp instantly, giving teams visibility into customer experiences across all locations.

After submitting their feedback, customers are always prompted to share their review publicly on platforms such as Google or TripAdvisor. The choice remains entirely theirs, ensuring reviews are authentic and policy-compliant.

If a customer leaves negative feedback, they can also indicate whether they would like to be contacted so the issue can be resolved privately before it escalates.

All feedback is brought together in a single dashboard, helping businesses track sentiment, identify trends per branch, and act early on potential issues.

About VoiceRevu

The Frustration

VoiceRevu started with a simple frustration. Too often, customers only speak up when they're already upset, and by then, the easiest place to vent is online. A bad review goes public, reputations take a hit, and the customer still doesn't feel heard. Nobody really wins.

Our Belief

We believe feedback should work differently. Most customers don't want to "destroy" a business online. They just want someone to listen, acknowledge the issue, and respond. VoiceRevu gives them a simple way to do exactly that, by speaking directly to management through WhatsApp, using a voice note or a message, in their own language.

The Solution

When customers share feedback this way, it feels more natural and more honest. Management sees it immediately and can respond while the experience is still fresh. Problems get resolved faster, misunderstandings are cleared up, and many issues never need to become public in the first place.

The Impact

After leaving feedback, customers are always given the option to share their experience publicly on platforms like Google or TripAdvisor. When the experience was positive, it's easy to amplify it. When it wasn't, there's a real chance to fix things first, and often keep a customer who would otherwise be lost.

The Mission

We built VoiceRevu because old methods don't work anymore. Email surveys and SMS links feel impersonal and are easy to ignore. People communicate differently now. WhatsApp is where real conversations happen, and modern AI allows us to turn those conversations into insights that businesses can actually use.

At the end of the day, VoiceRevu is about listening better.
About treating feedback as a conversation, not a score.
And about helping businesses learn, improve, and keep their customers, not lose them to a comment section.
