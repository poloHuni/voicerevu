Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Natnael Gebremichael. I studied Actuarial Science, but I've always been drawn to technology. When I started working with generative AI, I saw a chance to improve everyday tools we often take for granted.

The spark for Revu came one evening in a restaurant. I was asked to complete a feedback survey. It took 20 minutes. My food got cold, yet the survey still felt half done. I thought: there must be a better way.

That led me to design Revu. With our platform:

  • Customers can give voice or typed feedback via WhatsApp
  • You get all feedback in one clean dashboard
  • A bot can deliver answers or company info instantly

We want feedback to be effortless—for your customers and for you. Real, fast, and actionable.
