Revu Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 October 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Revu. We provide a service that lets businesses collect customer reviews via WhatsApp, and help customers share their feedback. Protecting your personal information is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

This policy applies to both the businesses using Revu and the customers sending reviews.

2. What information we collect

When you use Revu, we may collect:

From customers / message senders

  • Your phone number (the number from which you message)
  • The message text and any media (images, voice recordings, etc.)
  • The time your message was sent

From businesses

  • Business name, branch, industry
  • Business phone number
  • Business owner name
  • Business email
  • (Optional) Google Place ID or similar identifier

Review & analysis data

  • A cleaned or formatted version of the message for review (summary)
  • Overall sentiment or feedback in a simplified form
  • Per-group feedback or segmented content (e.g. service, food, atmosphere)
  • Key points (highlights)
  • Suggestions or recommendations

3. How we use your information

We use the information to:

  • Process and deliver customer reviews to the respective business
  • Analyze the feedback to produce summaries, highlights, and improvement suggestions
  • Help businesses understand their strengths and areas to improve
  • Communicate with you about service updates, support, or issues

By sending your review via WhatsApp, you consent to this use of your information.

4. How we protect your information

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • We transmit data securely (e.g. via encrypted communication)
  • Only authorized systems or people have access to the data needed for the service
  • We regularly review our security practices

While we strive for strong protections, no system can guarantee perfect security. If a breach occurs, we will act to notify affected parties where appropriate.

5. Sharing of information

We never sell personal data. We may share your information as follows:

  • With service providers we work with (for example, transcription or analytic tools)
  • With the business that is meant to receive the review (so they see relevant feedback)
  • If required by law or legal processes

We require that any third parties we share data with protect your information.

6. Data retention and deletion

We retain your information only as long as it is needed to deliver Revu's services, resolve disputes, or for legal obligations. Once it is no longer necessary, we will delete or anonymize it.

7. Your rights

You have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Accessing the data we hold about you
  • Correcting or updating inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Requesting deletion of your data (where possible)
  • Objecting to or restricting how we process your data
  • Withdrawing consent (if applicable)
  • Not receiving marketing or promotional messages

To exercise these rights, please contact us (see Section 9).

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Whenever we make changes, we will post the new version here with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Revu after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact us

If you have questions, concerns, or wish to exercise your privacy rights, you can reach us at:

  • Email: admin@voicerevu.com ; support@voicerevu.com

Thank you for trusting Revu with your feedback.
